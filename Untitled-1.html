Fact-Checking with Government Sources

What is Truely

Truely is a Chrome extension that makes accessing verified government information simple, fast, and reliable. By allowing users to highlight any claim or statistic online, Truely instantly fact-checks it against live data from official .gov.au sources, including Centrelink, ATO, and Medicare. It also provides real-time answers to user questions, helping Australians make confident, informed decisions without searching across multiple websites.

Why we need Truely

Australians often struggle with misinformation and conflicting advice online when searching for reliable details about finance, health, education, and government services. Official information from sources like Centrelink, ATO, and Medicare is often scattered, complex, and difficult to access, making it challenging to find accurate and up-to-date answers. Truely solves this by providing a Chrome extension that lets users highlight any claim, instantly fact-check it against verified .gov.au data, and receive real-time, trustworthy information.
